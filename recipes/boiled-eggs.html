Boiled Eggs Boiled Eggs

Here's a quick and easy to follow recipe for homemade boiled eggs!

Ingredients

  • 2 Whole White Eggs
  • A Pot
  • 1 Cup of Water


Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup of water into the Pot
  2. Place pot onto stove and let water come to a boil
  3. Once boiling, transfer eggs into water before of after boil (preferably before)
  4. Set a timer for 15 minutes whilst lowering heat to medium-high
  5. Turn off stove and set pot into sink to process, crack, and obtain boiled eggs
