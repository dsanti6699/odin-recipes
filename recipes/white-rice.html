White Rice White Rice

Here's a quick and easy to follow recipe for homemade boiled eggs!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Long Grain White Rice
  • A Pot
  • 1 Cup of Water


Steps

  1. Remove 1 cup of white rice and set into bowl; Proceed with cleaning rice by means of soaking water and leave for 2 minutes.
  2. Set 1 cup of water to boil on high flame.
  3. Strain water from bowl and set rice into pot.
  4. Allow rice and water to boil in pot, then set a cover (i.e. a lid).
  5. Lower flame to medium and allow rice to cook for 10 minutes; Once rice has cooked, stir in pot and set flame to low to allow for settle.
  6. As soon as rice is nice and fluffly, it's ripe for the taking!
